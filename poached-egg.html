Poached Egg

Description

With simple and inexpensive ingredients that you could find in your fridge at this very moment you have no excuse not to make this.

Ingredients

  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 cup water
  • Salt and pepper
  • 1 tomato optional

Steps

  1. Add 1/2 cup of water to small microwavable bowl.
  2. Crack egg into bowl.
  3. Place bowl into microwave for about 60-80 seconds, depending on the power of your microwave. As long as the egg doesn't explode, it will be okay.
  4. Remove bowl from microwave, and use a spoon (not a fork, duh) to remove the egg from the bowl (water not included).

  6. Take your favorite tomato, preferably a plump one, and cut it horizontally approximately 1/3 of the way down from the top. Scoop out the insides of the tomato to make it look like a bowl itself.
  7. Place egg in tomato, and voila! Bone apple tea! Your meal is complete, and now you can amaze your friends with your delicious looking egg entree.
